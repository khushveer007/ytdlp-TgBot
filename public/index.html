YT-DLP Telegram Bot

About this Bot

This Telegram bot allows you to download videos from various platforms using yt-dlp.

Simply send a video URL to the bot, select your preferred quality, and receive your download.

How to Use

  1. Search for the bot on Telegram
  2. Start a conversation with the bot
  3. Send a video URL from YouTube, Twitter, Instagram, etc.
  4. Select your desired video quality
  5. Wait for the download to complete

Supported Platforms

The bot supports many platforms including YouTube, Vimeo, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and more!
